About GrubDash

IMPORTANT NOTE: GrubDash is just a project for our IS 403 semester project. This is not a legitimate food delivery service. We do not fulfill orders. This is just a mock Grubhub and Doordash knockoff. We are not affiliated with those companies in any way. Now for the fake "About" information: GrubDash is your one-stop shop for all things food! Take a look through our numerous restaurants and order anything that will fulfill your cravings! Started in 2021, we made it our goal to give quick delivery to every hungry person on the block. Thanks for choosing GrubDash!
